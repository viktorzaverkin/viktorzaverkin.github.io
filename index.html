About

I am a Research Scientist at NEC Laboratories Europe in Heidelberg, Germany, specializing in the intersection of machine learning, chemistry, materials science, and physics. I earned my doctoral degree from the University of Stuttgart, where I introduced local equivariant atomic representation (Gaussian moments) [1, 2] and ensemble-free gradient-based uncertainty quantification methods [3, 4, 5]. Originally from Luhansk in eastern Ukraine.
